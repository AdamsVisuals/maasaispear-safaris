7-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Zanzibar

Airport transfer to your beach resort. Welcome drink and orientation. Evening at leisure to enjoy the beach.

Day 2: Stone Town Tour

Guided tour of historic Stone Town including the Old Fort, House of Wonders, and Darajani Market. Lunch at a local restaurant.

Day 3: Prison Island Excursion

Boat trip to Prison Island to see giant tortoises and snorkel in the coral reefs. Beach picnic lunch included.

Day 4: Relaxation Day

Day at leisure to enjoy resort amenities, spa treatments, or optional activities like diving or kayaking.

Day 5: Spice Tour

Visit to a working spice plantation to see how cloves, vanilla, cinnamon and more are grown. Includes traditional Swahili lunch.

Day 6: Jozani Forest

Explore Jozani Forest to see the rare red colobus monkeys. Afternoon free for last-minute shopping or beach time.

Day 7: Departure

Transfer to the airport for your flight home, taking memories of paradise with you.

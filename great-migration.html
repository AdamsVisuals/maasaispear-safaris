The Great Wildebeest Migration

Witness nature's greatest spectacle across the Serengeti and Maasai Mara

Best Time to Visit

The Greatest Show on Earth

Each year, over 1.5 million wildebeest, accompanied by hundreds of thousands of zebras and gazelles, complete a circular 1,200-mile journey through Tanzania and Kenya in search of fresh grazing and water.

This continuous movement creates one of the most dramatic wildlife experiences on the planet, with thrilling river crossings and predator interactions.

Migration Calendar

January - March

Southern Serengeti calving season - over 500,000 calves born

April - May

Movement northwest toward central Serengeti

June - July

Grumeti River crossings begin

August - October

Dramatic Mara River crossings in northern Serengeti

November - December

Return journey to southern Serengeti
